# Deleting Your Gita AI Account

Gita AI lets you delete your account and associated data at any time.

## Option 1 — In-app

1. Open Gita AI.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Tap "Sign out" to disconnect your Google account.
4. Tap "Clear chat history" to delete your chat messages from this device.

To fully delete the account record tied to your email, also follow Option 2.

## Option 2 — By email

Send an email to **atharva@innovisora.com** from the Google account you used to sign in, with the subject line "Delete my Gita AI account".

We will delete within 7 days:

- Your email, display name, and profile photo URL stored in Firebase
- Your subscription record
- Any cached AI-generated content linked to your account

Locally stored data (chat history, bookmarks) is never sent to our servers — it stays only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

We retain transactional records (purchase history) for 7 years as required by Indian tax law; these records contain no personal content beyond your Google Play account ID.
